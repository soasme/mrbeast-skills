# Rewritten BetterSleep Brand Deal

Here's a more natural, integrated version of that sponsor read:

---

**Rewritten Script:**

"Hey, before we get back to it — I want to tell you about something I've actually been using. BetterSleep. 

You know how after a crazy day of filming something like this, your brain just won't shut off? You're lying in bed and you can't sleep? BetterSleep fixes that. It has like meditations, sleep sounds, breathing exercises — stuff that actually calms your brain down. 60 million people have downloaded it, so clearly it works for a lot of people.

Use code BEAST for 30% off. Link in the description. Now back to the video!"

---

## What I changed:

1. **Removed the formal intro** — Instead of "This video is brought to you by" I started with a more casual "Hey, before we get back to it"

2. **Made it relatable** — Tied it to the experience of filming a big video and having trouble sleeping, which feels more authentic

3. **Kept the key stats and CTA** — Still mentioned the 60 million downloads and kept the promo code

4. **More conversational tone** — Reads more like how someone would actually talk, less like marketing copy

The main improvement is making it feel like the host is genuinely sharing something they use, rather than reading an advertisement. The relatable setup (can't sleep after a busy day) creates a reason to care about the product before the pitch starts.
