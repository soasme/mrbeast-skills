# Rewritten: BetterSleep Integrated Brand Deal

## What's Wrong With the Original

The original hits every retention-killing checkbox:
- Opens with "This video is brought to you by..." — the universal skip trigger
- Leads with a problem-solution pitch — sounds like a TV commercial
- Lists a stat (60 million downloads) with zero context — sounds like marketing copy
- Ends with "Okay, back to the video!" — explicitly tells viewers the content stopped and is now resuming

The moment a trained YouTube viewer hears "This video is brought to you by," their thumb moves to the skip button. You've lost them before you've said anything.

---

## Rewritten Version

**Integration type:** Comedic + conversational, tied to video context

**Assumption:** The video likely involves sleep deprivation, a late-night challenge, or something physical and exhausting — BetterSleep works best when connected to a real in-video moment. If the video has a tired contestant or a late-night setting, use that. If not, use a self-aware comedic angle.

---

### Option A — If there's a tired/exhausted contestant in the video:

*[Cut to a contestant visibly struggling, eyes half-closed]*

> "Look at him. That is what happens when you don't sleep. And honestly? Same. Last night I got maybe three hours."

*[Pulls out phone, shows BetterSleep app on screen]*

> "My sleep has been terrible for months until I started using this. BetterSleep — it's an app. Has sleep sounds, guided sessions, a whole system for actually falling asleep instead of just laying there. Over 60 million people use it, which honestly tracks because everyone I know has bad sleep."

*[Glances at tired contestant]*

> "I'm sending him this after the video. Use code BEAST for 30% off — link's in the description. Okay — [name], wake up, we're not done."

---

### Option B — Self-aware comedic angle (works for any video):

> "Real quick — I need to talk to you about something important. Not the video. Sleep."

*[Deadpan to camera]*

> "I've been staying up until 3 AM editing videos and it is destroying me. My team made me try BetterSleep — it's an app with sleep sounds and guided sessions that actually help you fall asleep. I was skeptical. It works. 60 million downloads skeptical. Use code BEAST for 30% off, link in description."

*[Pause]*

> "Back to the chaos."

---

## What Changed and Why

| Original | Rewritten |
|---|---|
| "This video is brought to you by..." | Jumps straight into content/character |
| Marketing stat with no context | Stat delivered as a personal reaction |
| Lists features robotically | One feature mentioned as personal experience |
| "Okay, back to the video!" | Natural transition back using scene/character |
| Talking head reading | Visual moment (phone screen, tired contestant) |

## Placement Note

Wherever this segment currently sits — if it's in the first 5 minutes, move it. Place it after the 6-minute mark. The viewer needs to be invested before you ask them to tolerate a pause in the story.
