# HydroMax Brand Deal for "I Survived 7 Days in the Sahara Desert"

## Placement

For a 13-minute video, I'd recommend placing the brand deal somewhere in the **middle of the video** — around the 5–7 minute mark. This gives viewers time to get invested before the ad, and you still have plenty of content after it. Some creators also do the sponsor at the end, but mid-video tends to get more impressions since not everyone watches to the end.

## The Segment

Since the video is about surviving in the desert, HydroMax is a really natural fit — staying hydrated in extreme heat is literally life or death. Here's what the segment could look like:

---

"This video is brought to you by HydroMax! 

Being out here in the Sahara, staying hydrated is the most important thing. I've been using HydroMax electrolyte packets throughout this challenge, and honestly they've been a lifesaver.

---

## Tips

- Film this in the desert environment so it feels authentic
- Hold up the packet and maybe mix it into your water bottle on camera
- Keep it energetic and genuine
- The fact that you're literally in the desert makes this sponsor feel very natural

HydroMax is a great fit for this video because dehydration is the core challenge, so it doesn't feel random or forced.
