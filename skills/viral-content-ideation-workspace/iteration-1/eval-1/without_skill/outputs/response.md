# 5 Viral YouTube Video Concepts: Rubber Duck Theme

Here are five YouTube video concepts centered around rubber ducks:

## Concept 1: Giant Rubber Duck Takeover

**Title:** I Replaced Everything In My House With Rubber Ducks

**Premise:** The creator buys hundreds of rubber ducks and replaces household items — fills the fridge with them, puts them in the laundry machine, covers the bed, fills the bathtub. Comedy reactions and chaos ensue.

**Wow factor:** Opening the front door to reveal a house completely yellow from floor to ceiling in every room.

**Format:** Stunt/comedy

**CTR score:** 3

---

## Concept 2: Rubber Duck Racing Competition

**Title:** We Had The Most Intense Rubber Duck Race Ever

**Premise:** Set up an elaborate track for rubber duck racing with obstacles, fans voting on their duck, and prize money for the winner.

**Wow factor:** A massive custom-built race track with water slides, obstacles, and a finish line with a crowd cheering.

**Format:** Competition

**CTR score:** 3

---

## Concept 3: Rubber Duck Art Challenge

**Title:** I Made A Portrait Of The Mona Lisa Using Only Rubber Ducks

**Premise:** Creator painstakingly arranges thousands of rubber ducks of different colors to recreate famous artwork. Time-lapse of the process.

**Wow factor:** The final overhead reveal shot where the Mona Lisa is clearly visible from thousands of ducks.

**Format:** Creative challenge

**CTR score:** 4

---

## Concept 4: Rubber Duck Survival Challenge

**Title:** I Survived 24 Hours On A Raft Made Entirely Of Rubber Ducks

**Premise:** Build a raft from rubber ducks and stay afloat on a lake for 24 hours, dealing with weather, hunger, and the ducks slowly deflating.

**Wow factor:** The raft holding the creator up on open water, fully visible from a drone shot overhead.

**Format:** Survival challenge

**CTR score:** 4

---

## Concept 5: World Record Attempt

**Title:** I Set The World Record For Most Rubber Ducks In A Bathtub

**Premise:** Attempt to break the Guinness World Record for rubber ducks in a single bathtub, going progressively larger until overflow.

**Wow factor:** The bathtub overflowing with thousands of rubber ducks spilling across the bathroom floor.

**Format:** Record attempt

**CTR score:** 3
